Félix de Moya Anegón is research professor at the Institute of Public Goods and Policies (IPP) from the Spanish National Research Council (CSIC). His academic interests include scientometrics, bibliometrics, research evaluation, and science policy, and he has published around 100 papers in these fields. He is SCImago Research Group’s main researcher, where he has led renowned bibliometric projects including SCImago Journal & Country Rank, SCImago Institution Rankings, and The Atlas of Science. De Moya is also an advisor on science policy issues for national organizations of science and technology and research institutions around the world.
