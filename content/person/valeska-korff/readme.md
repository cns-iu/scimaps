Valeska P. Korff is Junior Professor of Methods in Organizational and Administrative Research at the University of Potsdam, Germany. Her research focuses on ambiguities and contestations associated with processes of institutionalization—such as prevalent in the context of the rationalization of civil society—and the role of organizations as drivers and sites of these dynamics. She holds a PhD in sociology from the University of Groningen, The Netherlands.
