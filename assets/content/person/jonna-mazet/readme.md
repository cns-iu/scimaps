Jonna Mazet, DVM, MPVM, PhD, is a professor of Epidemiology and Disease Ecology and executive director of the One Health Institute at UC Davis where she focuses on global health problem solving, especially for emerging infectious disease and conservation challenges. Dr. Mazet is active in international One Health research programs, especially disease transmission among wildlife, domestic animals, and people and the ecological drivers for disease emergence. She has recently been elected to the National Academies' Institute of Medicine in recognition of her successful and innovative approach to emerging environmental and global health threats.
